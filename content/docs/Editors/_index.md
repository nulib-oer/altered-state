## Physical Assessment

- Does the intervention compromise the structural integrity or binding of the volume?
- Is the intervention reversible? e.g. clipped or tipped-in vs. permanently glued
- Does it obscure original text in a way that renders the source unusable?

## Content & Ethics

- Does the intervention meet the "Pedagogical Intent" (e.g., provides a counter-narrative or critique)?
- Is the content free of libel or hate speech?
- Has the student signed a Creative Commons/Usage agreement?

## Other Considerations

- Consider
  
    - Precedent set for future submissions?
    - Provenance, anonymity, or consent of original author?
    - Cross-departmental implications (Catalogers, Public Services)?
    - Long-term storage or conservation cost?
    - Community or student voice that should be heard first?

## Decision Matrix (Select One)

- Status A: Permanent Addition | Catalog and shelve. Item enters the permanent record.
- Status B: Temporary/Ephemeral | Retain for a defined term, then deaccession or archive surrogate.
- Status C: Rejected | Returned to the creator with written rationale.
