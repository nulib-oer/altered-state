## Worksheet 6 Text

### Goal

Define the boundary between "defacement" and "enrichment" to draft a Statement of Participatory Stewardship.

**We don't need to control/standardize guerilla publishing. That's part of the point of the form.**

### Other Considerations

**We don't need a full policy. We are users of the collection too, and realistically any user can *add* or *remove* elements. We don't need to adopt a punitive, top-down process for this. Realistically it's not enforceable anyway.**

### Justification

**We can also assume the 'author' of the alteration has made decisions about copyright/credit through the creation itself. Anonymity and openness are assumed unless otherwise indicated.**
